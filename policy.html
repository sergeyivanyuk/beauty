6-Hour Policy & 30% Free

ALL No-Shows, Last Minute Cancellation, and Schedule Changes made within 6 hours to your appointment time will incur a 30% fee regardless of the reasons. Please do so at least six business hours before your appointment time to avoid these fees. 10:00 AM appointments must be canceled six business hours in advance by 2:00 PM the previous day, not 4:00 AM on the day of the appointment. Please understand that we are appointment only and loss of one appointment has a big impact since technicians are tied to one customer at a time.

The 30% fee will vary from $15 — $43, depending on the service you book.
