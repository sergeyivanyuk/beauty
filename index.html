Welcome to Danna Beauty Inc

We offer personalized eyelash extensions, brow and nail services. Our team specializes in creating beautiful lashes and brows that will enhance your natural beauty. Being transparent with our customers allows us to understand each of our customers need and build a trust. Its our priority to find personalized solutions that best fit your needs.

What is most important for us is our customers. It is customer’s experience, satisfaction and convenience. We have made conscious efforts to provide you with the best service, options, simplicity and clarity..

We believe in empowering women, and those who identify themselves as women. Confidence matters. If our beauty services help any woman to feel more confident about themselves, we will be pleased to make further efforts and work hard.

Thank you for taking your time to read about us and hope to see you in person at one of our locations.
